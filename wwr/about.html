White Water Rafting

Welcome to the premier destination for whitewater rafting adventures. We offer guided river trips for all skill levels, combining heart-pounding rapids with breathtaking natural scenery to create unforgettable outdoor experiences.

History

Founded in 1994, our rafting company began with a single second-hand raft and a deep passion for the wild river rapids. Our founder, a lifelong river guide, wanted to share the thrill of whitewatter navigation with anyone seeking adventure. What started as a small weekend operation quickly gained a reputattion for safety, excitement, and unforgettable outdoor experiences.
